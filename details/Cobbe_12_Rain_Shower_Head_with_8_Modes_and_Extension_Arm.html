Cobbe 12" Rain Shower Head

Transform your daily shower into a moment of pure relaxation with the Cobbe 12" Rain Shower Head. This elegant and practical shower system combines a fixed rain shower head with an 8-mode handheld spray, giving you the flexibility to switch between a soothing rainfall shower and a precise handheld option. Whether you're washing your hair, showering children, or simply cleaning, the multiple spray settings cater to all your needs.

The included adjustable extension arm lets you position the shower head exactly where you want it, ensuring no obstruction and maximizing comfort. This setup is perfect for those who appreciate both style and function in their bathroom. Plus, the upgraded 3-way diverter valve prevents hose sagging and tangling, making your shower routine more seamless and enjoyable.

Designed with anti-clog nozzles, the Cobbe showerhead ensures a steady, strong water flow and promotes better hygiene. The durable chrome finish adds a modern and sleek appearance, complementing any bathroom decor. With an easy-to-install design featuring a standard G1/2 hose port, you can upgrade your shower without the need for professional assistance. This is an ideal choice for anyone looking to enhance their bathroom comfort and make their daily routine a little more pleasant.
